Soccer.com – UX & Homepage Redesign

Client: Soccer.com

Role: Lead Designer

Tools: Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator

Years: 2013–2023

For over a decade, I have led the design evolution of Soccer.com’s digital presence. This homepage redesign focused on reducing friction in the user journey, simplifying category navigation, and enhancing overall visual hierarchy for better product discoverability. With extensive A/B testing and user feedback, the design refined calls to action, prioritized key product lines, and aligned closely with seasonal campaigns to drive conversion.

UNC Lineberger x Soccer.com – Breast Cancer Awareness Campaign

Client: Soccer.com, UNC Lineberger Comprehensive Cancer Center

Role: Art Director, Designer

Tools: Photoshop, InDesign

Year: 2018

I donated my time and design skills to support the UNC Lineberger Cancer Center during Breast Cancer Awareness Month. This campaign aimed to raise funds and awareness through a collection of limited-edition pink gear. Assets included a responsive landing page, promotional graphics, and digital banners used across web, email, and social. All proceeds from the gear went directly to cancer research and patient support programs.
