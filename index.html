Gasoline & Leather

Client:Personal Project

Role: Web Designer, Art Director, Photographer

Year: 2023

Gasoline & Leather is a personal branding experiment that merges vintage motor culture with clean, editorial web design. Inspired by the craftsmanship and raw style of café racers and track legends, the site captures the beauty of speed, mechanics, and design history. Every element — from typography to color palette — was chosen to reflect a love of analog machines and authentic storytelling.

Nike Neymar Jr. LP — Limited Edition

Client: Nike (via Soccer.com)

Role: Web Designer, Art Director, Photographer

Year: 2023

This project showcases a one-page campaign site built for the Neymar Jr. limited edition cleats. The page design focused on storytelling through bold imagery and engaging content layout to highlight the collaboration between Neymar and Bruno Big. Photography and visual layout were tailored to maximize emotional and cultural impact for the audience.
